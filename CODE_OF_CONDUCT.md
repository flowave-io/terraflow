# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of Terraflow, pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to fostering an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy environment where contributors can collaborate productively.

---

## Our Standards

Positive behaviors include:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward others
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Offering and accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and apologizing when appropriate
- Focusing on what benefits the Terraflow community

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying community standards
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct consistently and fairly
- Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, code, or contributions that violate this Code
- Explaining moderation decisions when appropriate

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Terraflow community spaces and public spaces where individuals represent Terraflow officially (such as events, online channels, or when using an official Terraflow email address).

---

## Reporting

If you experience or see unacceptable behavior, or have any related concerns, you are encouraged to report it via email at **maxpeten@gmail.com**.

All reports will be treated seriously, reviewed promptly, and handled with discretion.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction

**Impact:** Minor inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A private, written warning from maintainers explaining why the behavior was inappropriate and what could change.

### 2. Warning

**Impact:** A violation through a single incident or repeated actions.

**Consequence:** A formal warning, which may include restrictions on interactions or privileges.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Impact:** Serious or repeated violations.

**Consequence:** Temporary ban from Terraflow community spaces. No interaction allowed with those affected or maintainers during the ban. Violations may result in a permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Impact:** Persistent harmful behavior, harassment, or repeated violations.

**Consequence:** Permanent removal from all public interaction in the Terraflow community.
