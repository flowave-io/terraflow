# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it through GitHub Security Advisory in the [Security](https://github.com/flowave-io/terraflow/security) tab.

Once we receive your report, we will investigate the issue and update you on the next steps. Following our initial response, we may request further information and will make every effort to keep you informed of our progress.

Please do not disclose the details of the issue publicly until we have had a chance to address it.

---

## Handling Security Reports

1. **Acknowledgement**: We will confirm receipt of your report.
2. **Assessment**: We will investigate the issue to understand its impact and severity.
3. **Resolution**: We will work to develop a patch or mitigation.
4. **Disclosure**: Once fixed, we will publicly disclose the vulnerability along with credit to the reporter (unless anonymity is requested).

---

Thank you for helping keep Terraflow secure! We appreciate your effort in making Terraflow better.
